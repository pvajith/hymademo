Specifications

Ultralite

  • Material: High-quality polycarbonate
  • Thickness: 2mm to 12mm
  • UV Protection: Advanced UV coating
  • Applications: Roofing, skylights, and greenhouses
  • Features: High impact resistance, thermal insulation, and lightweight
  • Colors: Clear, bronze, and custom colors

Tilara

  • Material: Durable polycarbonate
  • Thickness: 3mm to 10mm
  • Weather Resistance: Excellent performance in extreme conditions
  • Applications: Industrial projects and architectural designs
  • Features: Lightweight, flexible, and easy to install
  • Colors: Transparent, opal, and blue

Polymac

  • Material: Economical polycarbonate
  • Thickness: 2mm to 8mm
  • Impact Resistance: High resistance to shocks
  • Applications: DIY projects, partition walls, and glazing
  • Features: Cost-effective, durable, and versatile
  • Colors: Frosted, smoke, and green

Eurolite

  • Material: Premium-grade polycarbonate
  • Thickness: 4mm to 12mm
  • Aesthetic Appeal: Enhanced clarity and gloss
  • Applications: Modern architectural projects and interiors
  • Features: Scratch-resistant, UV-protected, and long-lasting
  • Colors: Customizable with high gloss finish

Arolite

  • Material: Industrial-grade polycarbonate
  • Thickness: 5mm to 15mm
  • Light Transmission: Up to 90%
  • Applications: Warehouses, factories, and greenhouses
  • Features: Weatherproof, low maintenance, and robust
  • Colors: Clear, grey, and white
