Starwood WPC planks

Overview

Starwood decorative WPC planks exhibit magnificent pre-finished wood looks for exterior and interior applications. Weatherproof and highly durable, Starwood WPC planks require no painting or maintenance and come with a 10-year anti-fading warranty.

Surface on One Plank

Starwood WPC planks have 2 surface finishes on the same plank, presenting true characteristics of finished natural wood. One side is engrained like natural wood and the other, a machine finished grooved surface. With this feature, Starwood offers flexibility in designing for different applications.
