How Turbo Ventilator Works

Turbo ventilators operate on wind energy. The wind causes the ventilator to spin, creating a centrifugal force that draws up hot air from inside the building and expels it outside. This helps in maintaining a cooler and more comfortable environment inside. The ventilator continues to operate even with minimal wind, providing continuous ventilation without the need for electricity.
